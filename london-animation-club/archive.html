London Animation Club at Hong Kong Contemporary 2013

London Animation Club has produced a one-hour programme of outstanding animations for Hong Kong Contemporary 13 (HKC13), an annual international art fair at the Excelsior Hotel in Hong Kong, from 24th to 27th May 2013. The programme features work by seventeen of the club's core members and is the club's first screening outside London. For those unable to attend HKC13, you can watch the LAC programme on Vimeo:

One of the many highlights of the 22-film programme, curated by LAC founder Martin Pickles, is Act Of Terror by Gemma Atkinson, which details her experience of being handcuffed and detained by police for filming them on her mobile phone. The film was facilitated by LAC by introducing Gemma to animator Una Marzorati. The film got 150,000 views on its first day on The Guardian website.

The programme also features Exquisite Corpses, LAC's first group project. Each participant has made a four-second animation, with the last frame of each section becoming the first frame of the next. The result is a Surrealist comic maelstrom, starring Neil Armstrong, Emmeline Pankhurst and Boris Johnson.
